Queering Poetry: A Soft Gathering

This event has been a long-time dream of mine— and i'm using my birthday to pilot it. It’s a (preferably sober) space for anyone who loves words, whether you want to share your own poetry, read someone else’s, or simply listen. No experience is needed—this is a place to put the words usually tucked away in your notes app. Bring your poems, favorite lyrics, or anything that speaks to you. A queer space where allies are more than welcome. No one is "too much" of a beginner, come as you are. Click on the invite to find out info.

*Queering: reinterpreting, or expanding traditional norms, perspectives, or structures, often through the lens of LGBTQ+ experiences or identities.
